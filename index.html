About Blue Ocean

We focus on marine conservation, plastic reduction, and protecting endangered ocean species.

Our Mission

Blue Ocean is dedicated to restoring marine habitats, promoting sustainable fishing, and reducing ocean pollution through global awareness programs.

Founded in 2015, we now have over 80,000 volunteers across 60 countries working together to make oceans cleaner and safer.
